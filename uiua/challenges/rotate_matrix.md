Write a function that rotates a two-dimensional array (matrix) 90 degrees clockwise.

### Example

**Input:**

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9
\end{bmatrix}
$$

**Output:**

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
7 & 4 & 1 \\
8 & 5 & 2 \\
9 & 6 & 3
\end{bmatrix}
$$

### Explanation:

Each row of the matrix becomes a column in the rotated version, with the first row becoming the last column.
